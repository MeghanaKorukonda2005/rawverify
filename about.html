About Integrity Hub

Building trust in construction supply chain with AI + Blockchain

Our Idea

Integrity Hub is a transparent marketplace that connects construction material buyers (contractors, government projects) with sellers (suppliers). Our focus is on removing middlemen, ensuring materials are genuine, and making payments secure with UPI / RuPay.

How to Use

  1. Buyer requests a product (Cement, Steel, Sand, Bricks, etc.).
  2. Seller uploads product photos with geo-tags and live webcam proof.
  3. Our AI scanner checks quality (Best / Good / Average / Poor).
  4. Buyer verifies and places a bulk order (200–1000kg).
  5. Payment is done in installments via UPI / RuPay.
  6. Order is delivered to site and logged for accountability.

Our Vision

We aim to make construction projects transparent, fair, and accountable. By combining AI-powered material verification, blockchain-based logs, and digital payments, we reduce corruption and ensure quality for public infrastructure.
